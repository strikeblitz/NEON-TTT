// PRIVACY & POLICY

DATA COLLECTION

Your username, hashed password, profile picture URL, and game statistics (wins/losses/draws) are stored in our cloud database. No personal identifying information beyond your chosen username is collected.

PROFILE PICTURES

Profile pictures you upload are stored publicly via Catbox.moe, a third-party image hosting service. Do not upload sensitive or private images. If you choose not to upload, a generated avatar is used.

LOCAL STORAGE

Your session credentials and notification read-state are stored in your device's local storage for auto-login. Clear your browser data to remove these.

ONLINE GAMEPLAY

Online matches use WebRTC peer-to-peer connections. Your IP address may be visible to your opponent during connection negotiation via STUN servers (Google & Cloudflare).

GUEST MODE

Playing as a guest stores no data on our servers. Game results in guest, local, and AI modes are not counted toward leaderboard stats.

CONTACT

For data removal or questions, contact the developer via Telegram at @blitzXdev.
